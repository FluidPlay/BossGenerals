- Differently from normal Generals points, that enable one power or extra unit, Boss Generals player's early strategy is defined by his General Plan - "Genplan". This first point will enable upgrades, new units and different unit and structure behaviours, totally changing the gameplay for both you, general, and your opponent - as soon as he realizes which genplan you picked, which can be too late! Be warned that, although you can save the early point to spend on a normal general point later on, refusing to use the incredible power of the genplans since the very early game may prove extremely hard and cost you games.


Advanced Infantry

The infantry genplan is for the general who values his manpower first and foremost. Having minigunners and nuke tank hunters at his disposal makes having less powered tanks a small price to pay. The minigunners can be equipped by the feared new hellgun, a portable flamethrower that can scatter his flames in an arc, and the Tank Hunter earns new nuke missiles with extra damage and able to create nuclear fields when hitting the same spot in numbers. The nuke missiles have twice the resistance of normal missiles, taking two PDL lasers to be destroyed and being therefore an invaluable tool to face heavy american PDL technology. Both weapon improvements can be achieved researching the 'advanced weapons' upgrade, that's enabled by this genplan. If that wasn't enough, the infantry-loving Boss China general will have Assault Troopcrawlers - carrying 6 minigunners - at his disposal.

Technical Details:
- Advanced Weapons upgrade buildcost $900, research time 45s (barracks)
- Minigunner buildcost $370
- Advanced Troopcrawler buildcost $2,400
- Nuke missiles rate of fire increased by 20%, ground range 10% higher, 5 THs create a small nuke field

Combos:
- Bunker Helix + 5 nuke missile tank hunters - Extra powerful vs GLA
- Advanced Weapons + Minigunner Drop - burn to ashes your opponent unprotected spots
- Bunker Overlord w/ nuke missile tank hunters + ECMs - The ultimate ground owneror
- 1 Assault Troopcrawler with 4 minigunners and 2 THs

 

 

Blitzkrieg

Generals that like to raid the opponent base before he's fully prepared and still deliver maximum (many times lethal) damage will seriously consider 'Blitzkrieg' his genplan of choice. Kang's Attack Outpost (AO) is a slightly less powered version of the much feared Attack Outpost, being more vulnerable to tank fire, but well managed will prove itself an excellent flanking and supporting weapon to your attack force. To complete its firepower, count on the new and improved Gattling Tank Tao chainguns. With Tao, the gattling tank not only gain extra tank armor piercing damage, but most importantly it gains superior ground range and anti-structure power. A solid force of AOs and Tao-GTs can not only break through the enemy lines, it can rip his base apart in seconds.

Technical Details:
- Support Outpost comes with 3 Tank Hunters, buildcost $1000, repair time 2/4 s
- Tao Chainguns upgrade cost is $1,500, research time 40s (Warfactory)
- Tao Chaingun gattling tank ground range is 175 (vs 165 normal), ramps up faster

Combos:
- 2 AOs + 2 GTs w/ Pierce chainguns - GTs rape infantry, AOs clean up the rest, evacuate Tank Hunters when needed for maximum efficiency
- AOs + Red Guards - Put the red guards into the AOs for anti infantry power
- AOs + Nuke Tank Hunters - Advanced Infantry + Blitzkrieg = Nuke Attack Outposts!

 

 


Nuclear Tanks

 

Who believes only in the brutest force tend to think nothing can really beat an uber tank in the battlefield - and won't think twice before choosing Nuclear Tanks as his genplan. The Nuclear powered tanks (Battlemaster and Overlord) not only move way faster, they also fire more powerful radiation shells. A side effect of their nuclear power is their instability in the battlefield, meaning that when they blow up they damage whatever is around them - even friendly stuff. Recent China technology have reduce the radiation that used to spill from the engine causing the massive explosions, still for total safety the 'isotope stability' upgrade still has to be researched. The Nuclear Tanks genplan is also responsible for unlocking the mighty "neutron radiation" upgrade for Neutron Cannons, 50% stronger and able to evacuate buildings.

Technical Details:
- Nuclear Tanks require activation in the power plant (free, instant)
- Isotope Stability cost is $1,000, research time 40s (Nuclear Silo)
- Neutron Radiation cost is $800 per Neutron Launcher
- 2 Nuke BMs + 1 Adv Troopcrawler w/ 3 THs - Powerful and fast combo, put THs into crawler to add anti-tank and anti-air protection to the fast moving detection unit
- 2 Nuke BMs + 1 Gattling Tank + 1 Dragon - Armor, anti-infantry and anti-structure combo

 


Emperor Tank
 ***

 

The Emperor Tank is very different from the standard Overlord tank in Boss Generals. First, it sports a full-rate gattling and detection weapon, which makes the standard overlord gattling pale in comparison both in detection range and rate of fire. The Emperor Tank is inherently stronger than the original version, and the subliminal message upgrade empowers them enormously. Emperor Tanks have the standard Tank armor, which's more resistant to infantry missiles than the special tank armor that revests the Nuke Battlemaster and Standard Overlord.

Minigunner Paradrop
***
  

Drops 3 Minigunners anywhere on the map. Outstanding strategic weapon, specially when the hellgun upgrade (Infantry weapons) is purchased, which allows the MGs to work as flame infantry.


Emergency Repair
*****

  

A lesser version of the USA heavy repair general point, it also recharges faster and it's available quite early in the battles, providing for a healthy boost in the China army when picked, specially useful in harrassment mission or to extend key units life long enough to make them do their jobs. Boss China Emergency repair is much faster than the original armies version.


Artillery Barrage 2
***

  

Equivalent to the original level 2 Artillery Barrage, it's as powerful and obtained sooner. Very useful in clearing a target area defense right before moving in with an army, or as a tactical support to soften up heavily defended areas. Specially useful against EMP patriot clusters.

 


Boss Hero Power
Seduce
*****

  The Boss Hero power that "turn you on"! Seduce is the new Lotus ability of convincing enemy vehicle pilots into turning to the China side. This slick and unresistable woman trick is so sneaky that the Super Lotus agent doesn't even decloaks itself while using it. Has devastating effects when captures a highly lethal unit like an Emperor, a Dragon Tank or one upgraded Marauder. Can easily turn the tide of battles since it recharges fast. Seduce is highly useful to capture enemy american dozers, opening their powerful technology tree for China.

Tank Paradrop
*****
  Two battlemasters are dropped anywhere on the battlefield. Extremely damaging for solo infiltration missiles, it also serves as an outstanding free armor boost to your forces. Dropped battlemasters share the same upgrades of all battlemasters, making this general power even more interesting considering the long-run power of upgrade-stacked Boss Battlemasters.


Tactical Nuke Mig
*****

  Enables the $1,500 Tactical Nuke Mig upgrade in the Internet Center. Differently from older versions, the Black Mig is able to stack its damage types, therefore it uses a napalm-nuke missile that in packs can create a firestorm *and* a nuclear field at the same time - making this the most cost-effective air weaponry ever.


Rage frenzy
*****

   Rage Frenzy is a long lasting version of the Frenzy 2 original power, not only able to power up the units weapon under its effect, but also the reload rate of those weapons.

EMP Cluster Mines
*****
  No simple mines here, these are EMP mines able to be deployed anywhere on the map and de-occupy vehicles that inadvertedly run over it. Invaluable for choke points and as a primary defense in flanks or heavy traffic areas, is very effective when dropped in front of enemy war factories synced with a major invasion, trapping any reinforcement vehicle that gets out of the war factory.

Fast EMP Pulse
*******
  Disabling a full army was never easier with the new EMP Pulse, carried by a much faster cargo plane than the original version, and reloading 1 minute faster. Always available wherever you need it, at a blink of an eye, and will instantly disable tanks to allow you to merrily crush them with Boss China Overlords and Emperors.

Carpet Bomb
*******
  Furious and fast recharging General Power, the Nuke Carpet Bomb is one of China enemies' worst nightmare, being as obliterating to structures as it is to vehicles, which throws in the air amidst its deadly nuke explosions.

Battlemaster Autoloader
*******
  The ultimate tank upgrade could be available to no one faction other than the world-renoumned Tank firepower masters. That's not a simple "add damage" upgrade as the original, mind you. It seriously speed up the reload rate of the tank turret, and I mean seriously. The firing rate of subliminal-autoloader battlemasters is absolutely outrageous, some people has been said to confuse totally upgraded battlemasters under subliminal and rage frenzy influence with Gattling Tanks!
