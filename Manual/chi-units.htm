Nuke Tank Hunter

The Boss Tank Hunter, albeit a costly infantry unit, got at the peak of the man-portable weaponry technology, with the incredible nuke-tip missiles able to create nuke fields when in numbers aimed at the same spot. Tank Hunters are inherently more resistant to nuclear radiation, and can also be equipped with armored vests making them extra resistant in the battle field.


Red Guard

The "numbers" unit in all China armies couldn't have a different role for Boss China. Improved training techniques assure their veteran status since the first battle.

Super Hacker

Stealthed. Faster moving. Quicker vehicle disabling weapon. Faster veterancy status. Although not exactly cheap, the Boss Super Hacker advantages mean it's the best Hacker ever for a China army, that will give you more cash in the long run and also work wonders as a support unit against overwhelming armies.

 

Super Lotus

The most hated infiltration and spying unit for enemy armies, Boss Super Lotus is meaner and nastier than ever, with super-fast structure capture, sleeker movement in the battlefield and the unique ability of seducing enemy vehicles into turning to the China side. Just guess what a seduced enemy Dragon Tank in the middle of his base can do...


Minigunner
(genplan)

The once specialized fast-spinning gunner is now an even more powerful unit, with its new Hellgun weapon. Able to switch from normal minigun to flame mode with this add-on, Boss Minigunners in numbers are know to quickly level enemy structures, and are a serious threat to unprotected enemy outposts and even main base when dropped by planes.

Activated by: Advanced Infantry Genplan

 


Nuke Battlemaster
(genplan)

Boss Battlemaster basic engine is faster than the original Battlemaster one. New fast-assemble technology assures that the Battlemaster is the faster building tank known to mankind. Turned into Nuke BMs they become deadly war machines, extremely hard to counter. Expect many impressive upgrades for the Battlemaster, a fully upgraded Battlemaster is known to rival even standard overlords in terms of raw firepower.

Activated by: Nuclear Tanks Genplan


Advanced Troopcrawler

The Advanced Troopcrawler is key to Boss China impressively aggressive tactics. Starts with 8 red guards, but differently from its predecessor, the ATC has fireports that allow carried troops to fire from within, working as the perfect hero defense. Coupled with a twin-engine and reinforced armor, the ATC can not only pack a severe punch loaded with the amazing nuke-RPGs Tank Hunters, it can now outrun even a gattling tank.

Dragon Tank

The new China fast-assemble technology improved the Dragon Tank construction time, making it an even more handy unit for the Boss China arsenal, and reinforcing its stablished reputation for counter-rushes and quick base leveling tasks.

 

Inferno MK2

Critics of the original Inferno Cannon design had many reasons to complain. Original Inferno Cannons, although less cumbersome than Nuke Cannons, had such a slow moving projectile that they could rarely fulfill their anti-infantry role. Add to that the high technology requirements to build them (Propaganda Center), and you might as well give these detractors complete reasoning. General Kang, a well known lover of fire weapons, wasn't shy at all when he personally projected the brand new Inferno MK2. With increased turret pressure, faster reload and good firestorm-creation abilities, his fire launcher technical components were also simplified to make their assembling possible in standard War Factories.


Gattling Tank

 Historically feared by enemy generals by its raw brute force, Boss China new blitzkrieg philosophy turned the gattling tank amazing efficiency in the ultimate battlefield mobile chaingun weapon. It's hard to describe how amazingly powerful its new pierce chaingun weapon is, you gotta see to believe it - worse yet, fear it!


Assault Troopcrawler
(genplan)

In practice an old-fashioned troopcrawler (ie. less armored and slower compared to the advanced version) that comes with 6 minigunners that can fire from within, the ATC has always been a feared anti-air/detection unit, and with its crew's new weapon, the hellgun, enemies have yet another big reason to fear it.

Enabled by: Advanced Infantry genplan


ECM Tank

The Boss ECM tank is the same ever-needed basic China missile-deflection support unit, but latest production models are know to have some range limitation in their vehicle disabling weapon. Some new cutting-edge enemy weapons are known to make his deflection shield to work erratically, namely vs Black Napalm missiles, the EP raptor missile and the GLA's MAPATs RPG missile. The China general can always add extra ECMs to his fleet to better protect against these threats though.

Overlord Tank

Boss 'Ollies' are natively equipped with nuclear engines, assuring great mobility in the battlefield. To keep production costs low, it can only be equipped by a slower spinning version gattling weapon version, which also has a shorter detection rate than other tank-assembled gattlings. Nevertheless, once equiped with Nuclear Tank weapons it has astounding anti-infantry power - due to radiation spill from shells - and even faster speed.

Attack Outpost
(genplan)

Due to a policy of focusing investments on tank technology, the Boss Attack Outpost is a slightly less armored version of the original Attack Outpost, being more vulnerable to tank fire. Well managed it quickly proves itself an excellent flanking and supporting anti-tank weapon to your attack force. The Nuke-RPG upgrade also give it a huge boost in damage and anti-infantry power.

Enabled by: Blitzkrieg genplan


Neutron Cannon

The portable nuclear weapon vehicle of the China army is better than ever. The Neutron Cannon replaced the old Nuke Cannon, sporting the new Neutron Radiation Shells - available with the Nuclear Tanks genplan. This upgrade costs $600, which's a lot considering it's done per neutron cannon, which already costs $1,600. Still, albeit reloading sensibly slower than the original weapon, the neutron radiation weapon is able to deliver massive anti-structure damage. Reports relate it's able to destroy a war factory with a single hit, just imagine what it's able to do to a column of tanks.


Emperor Tank
(genplan)

The Emperor Tank is not only the toughest tank in existance, it can heal itself with a built-in propaganda tower and still get a steath-detection gattling weapon at the same time. Boss China Emperor Tanks had their control computer systems updated so they take full advantage of the China Subliminal Messaging upgrade, making it even more sturdy and helpful in the battlefield.

Enabled by: Emperor Tank genpoint

 

 


Black Mig

The Black Mig-46 is the China response to the recent history of America's claim on air superiority. As fast and cunning as earlier models, the new mig has a much higher field of view, making it an excellent scouting unit and giving it better time to react to incoming moving anti-air threats. Its weapon systems were totally reworked from the very basics, with faster and way more accurate missiles that are able to carry the unique China dual-charge projectile: the Nuke-Napalm missile, able to create a devastating Fire Storm and a Nuclear explosion at the same time. The Black Napalm armed missiles, although now taking longer to load in the aircrafts, have been redesigned to withstand double the damage from the american anti-missile weapon, the PDL, making Migs once again able to dogfight on more equal terms with the US airforce. Also of special note is the new "Mig Armor and flare" upgrade, able to fire a single missile countermeasure flare on each flight, beyond enhancing the armor plating of the aircraft.


Helix S2

Already feared by the opponents by its amazing flexibility, Helixes have been deemed by many generals in the red army as cumbersome and limited units. Massive investments later, the Helix S2 model came to life, addressing most of the concerns of the earlier model. It now has a default long-ranged propaganda turret, able to not only heal nearby units but also to increase his overall re-usability on the battlefield by quickly healing itself. An awesome nuke-penetration bomb - supposedly able to clear garrissoned buildings - has been added as an optional weapon, and as expected it can also be equipped by a troop-carrier bunker or a gattling turret, completing its vast arsenal.
