Q - What do I need to run Boss Generals?

A - Eletronic Arts' Command and Conquer™ Generals and its expansion pack, Zero Hour, installed on your computer. Optionally, the "Ultimate Warfare Pack", or "Deluxe Edition", which are software bundles with both the game and its expansion. It's currently sold at very affordable prices, check here.

Q - What are the system requirements?

A - The basic system requirements are the same as the original, PIII 800 Mhz with 256 Megs RAM. Nevertheless, Boss Generals' games tend to last longer and have larger, more intense battles than the original - and that requires some horsepower. Optimally we recommend at least a 1.6 Ghz CPU (AMD or Intel) with a Radeon 7500, Nvidia FX5200 or better video card and 512 Megs RAM. All drivers should be updated for best performance.

Q - Can I play online?

A - Sure, that's the main objective of Boss Generals, providing a blasting multiplayer experience. You can play normally on EA's GOL (Generals Online) system. Games created with the Boss mode activated will have their names shown up as white, others will show up greyed out.

Q - What if I want to play the original game mode, do I have to uninstall Boss?

A - Certainly not. There's an easy-to-use "loader" software bundled in the Boss Generals installation that makes loading the game with the standard or Boss gameplay modes a simple button click away.

Q - Does it have new single player missions?

A - No, but due to the own nature of the modification balance rules, you'll find playing the standard missions an extremely challenging task. So you can consider we provide a "Boss Hard" difficulty mode that should make it very pleasant playing the single player missions again. We have developed a rich Boss Generals storyline, which might turn into a full-blown Boss campaign mode at the proper time.

Q - Is the AI as weak as the original?

A - The original AI wouldn't even be able to play the Boss mode, due to the huge differences between both. That's why MaD-X Studios developed a custom "Boss Hard" AI that's quite challenging to take even for experienced players (Aggr0, 10 times Clanwars Champion, has reported being crushed by it in his first tries). The Boss Hard AI provides for a very good training ground for Boss Generals before you engage on real battles against live opponents.

Q - I can't find some basic stuff, like how to activate the nuclear tanks plan for China, my tanks are still crawling..

A - Due to some engine limitations, not every genplan benefit could be made to be fully automated, requiring one extra click in some specific structure to have units upgraded. To find how a specific genplan or power is fully activated, please refer to our comprehensive "Boss Armies", army powers section. In this specific case, the Nuclear Tanks genplan is activated for free at the China power plant.

Q - Where can I find replays of Boss Generals games?

A - Gamereplays.org for sure! Go to our "forum" section, there's a link that'll take you straight to the Boss Generals forums, where you'll find many 1v1 and team replays for Boss Gens.
