Toxin Rebel

GLA's recent toxic bio-technology research results made the toxic power increase surpass the toxic resistance of their own soldiers. A small price to pay for the extra power, according to the Jackal. Boss GLA's basic infantry, the toxin rebel, is therefore not entirely invulnerable to toxins, but still highly resistant to it, and can as easily clean up garrissoned structures as ever. Toxin rebels riding bikes instantly gain the gamma upgrade to their beta weapon. Feeling the need for more artillery support for his ground-only based forces, the Jackal equipped the Toxin Rebels with Molotov Cocktails as soon as he aquires the demo tools science, with a marginal cost per rebel. Although very innacurate, Molotov Cocktails have a range similar to the China's Inferno Cannon, and deliver great anti-structure damage, therefore completing the Toxin Rebels usefulness - specially when camouflaged while moving across the battlefield or loaded into a battlebus.


RPG Trooper

The always feared GLA's rocket-propelled-grenade soldier is now even more threatening. Due to a behind-the-curtains agreement with some corrupted China secret agents, the RPG soldier weapon can be trained faster, meaning a faster production. The real key to its extra power though, is the new extra-resistance Gamma RPG missile, able to overload enemy PDL and ECM protection devices to some extent. MAPATs technology has also evolved, and now equips these portable missiles after upgraded, adding range and power to the already devastating RPGs. You will *not* want to see a huge mass of fully upgraded RPGs coming at your base!

Demo Terrorist

Highest symbol of the GLA disattatchment to life when pursuing their "freedom" objectives, the Boss GLA's terrorist can be equipped with much more powerful explosives after the "Demo Terror" upgrade - available with the Demo Tools genplan - is purchased. A small $250 entry fee for 33% extra powerful explosives. Jackal's love for terrorist actions didn't stop there though - the Demo Terrorist is even more boosted by the Gamma upgrade, leaving a gamma toxin puddle after his suicidal attack and getting a whooping 50% increase in damage over the basic explosives he carries, becoming the most powerful and fearsome terrorist ever.

 

Tech Saboteur

The once somewhat ridiculed GLA infiltration soldier is now a highly valued part of the Boss GLA arsenal. Due to intense training and some technology stolen from american captured scout drones, the Tech Saboteur is now equipped with a long-range stealth detection device in his backpack. Way faster and able to steal double the cash from enemy supplies than the first saboteurs, the Tech Saboteur is a serious contender for best Boss GLA support unit.

 

Jarmen "Demo" Kell
 It's well know that the heroic Jarmen Kell is the undisputed #1 "concentration breaker" for enemy armies, and well used he can easily turn the tide of battles towards the terror side. Always worried about his speed on the battlefield though, "Mr Demo" swapped the belt space he once used for his stealth-detection device by a new compact flare-firing system that is known to signal the target of a new and scary GLA strategy designed to demoralize and terrify the enemy.


Stealth Hijacker
(genplan)

 Boss Stealth Hijacker is always camouflaged, even while moving. Now, beyond his traditional ability of hijacking enemy tanks, the stealth hijacker can receive a special training that makes him able to infiltrate and take control of enemy buildings. Infiltration speed is average, 15 seconds, and its only downside is being only useable at melee range, meaning the infiltrating Hijacker should fear any form of mine.

Militia Mob
(genplan)
The once almost-uncontrollable angry mobs that GLA incited to the war was power-packed but was way too slow and open to aerial threats. Soon Jackal decided to start gathering members of the people willing to fight, and properly equipped and trained them. Since leaving their training camp, the Militia Mob is armed with special AK47s with average accuracy against air targets, albeit with a shorter range than vehicle-based AA. Through special training, advanced special Militia Mobs has been reported as able to perform ambushes in unexpected places and even travel across the GLA tunnel networks!

 


Technical

How can a frail unarmored pickup with a low-caliber mounted machinegun be so devastating in the hands of the best GLA generals? That certainly wasn't the smallest doubt in the mind of the terrorist mastermind, Jackal, when he kept the Technical as traditional as the original specifications as ever. However, not being the number 1 priority in the Boss GLA army anymore, it's been reported as slower to build, although still even more capable to surprise and quickly put the enemy in a "check mate" position, due to the enhanced Boss GLA's infantry and unguessable genplan and upgrades taken.


Quad Cannon

The Boss Quad Cannon keeps the basic characteristics that made it the most produced vehicle ever in the recent GLA history, but it now has a much improved performance when upgraded with Turbo AP bullets. This new system allows it to fire at a much higher rate and with a special anti-air bullet. These fast-spinning Quadruple machineguns are quite a sight to be seen - or sound to be listened, since it's been described as terrifying!


Rocket Buggie

Fastest long-range moving unit known by mankind, the Boss Buggie has an even superior performance than its previous version, with the MAPATs upgrade that extend its range and damage power noticeably.

Battle Bus

Jackal intended the Battle Bus to have an almost 50% more resistant internal armor, to help keeping the infantry surving - and firing - longer after the bus wheels had been destroyed. Some compromises had to be done to achieve that, and the final result is a heavier, slower bus than its predecessors. Intelligence reports that the external armor has been made lighter to alleviate some of the new weight, which should render this new battlebus version more vulnerable to infantry missiles and direct tank hits. Bear in mind though, that with the help of armor scraps gathered from enemy destroyed vehicles or from parachute-dropped crates, the Boss Battle Bus can even exceed the original Battlebus resistance specs.

 


Toxin Marauder Tank
(genplan)

With the recent investments in faster technology aquisition (through reversed enginnering), GLA's is finally able to produce Marauders at a faster rate. A modern fast-turning turret substituted its old fixed turret, and now sporting some impressive special devices like built-in toxin shells and a "decoy" hidden compartment able to unfold and control a decoy copy of the tank itself while in the battlefield, the brand new Toxin Marauder is been seen by the enemies as the most impressive technological advancement for the GLAs armies ever.


Scorpion Tank
(genplan)

 As handy and cunning as ever, the fast-and-cheap Scorpion Tank can now be equipped by a 20% more powerful rocket than the old scorpion rocket - the new 'Demo Rocket'. Damage to large enemy tanks is outstanding, although when unequipped by that weapon the scorpion is still not resistant enough to face sizeable enemy armies. Toxin Shells were discontinued for this tank model, considered a costly redundance to the new demo 'red' rockets.


Radar Van

As much as the Tech Saboteur is a quantum leap forward in GLA stealth detection technology, the Radar Van also wasn't left in the dust. After the camouflage general power is activated, all radar vans become stealthed when not moving, making harder for the enemy to track and destroy them, and giving no hints to enemy basic scout if the Boss GLA general decides to use massed vans with the radar scan upgrade.

Terror Bike
(genplan)
The Terrorist Combat Cycle, knicknamed as Terror Bike by the opponents, is a fast moving motorcycle able to climb hills and jump over regular cliffs to quickly carry its rider to his destiny. Known as the best surprise weapon of the GLA for its fast and unpredictable routes of attack, it's quite invaluable also with a jarmen kell on top of it. Boss GLA combat cycle has a vastly improved RPG dock, that makes the RPG weapon fire with a longer range than usual and it's specially powerful with the MAPATs damage and range upgrade.


Toxin Tractor

Boss Toxin Tractor already starts with Anthrax Beta, and becomes increasingly high ranged and armored when upgraded with scraps. As the legendary - officially MIA - General Kassad's Toxin Tractor, Boss' tractors also gain impressive anti-tank damage with the gamma upgrade. Bear in mind that the "special tanks" (battlemaster, overlord and scorpion) are twice as vulnerable to toxins than the standard Tanks.

Scud Launcher
(genplan)
Jackal has always been a firm believer that no artillery system in the world is better than the GLA one, simply because you can't hit what isn't there - and that's exactly the power of scuds popping in and out of tunnels. Although China and US created powerful devices to deviate the trajectory and even destroy the scuds in mid-air, which somewhat affected this powerful weapon reputation, Jackal developped a way to inject the armor and advanced guidance system of enemy captured devices into the Boss Scud Launcher missile. Scrap-upgraded scud missiles get tougher and guided, but double-scrapped scuds become practically invulnerable to PDL and ECM defensive measures. Also, Boss Scud Launchers are the first ever to integrate the gamma and high explosion damages in the same weapon, reinforcing the fact that these are the most devastating scud lauchers ever.

Armored Combat Cycle
 This unit's existance is controverted, but it's been reported by spies that Jackal developed a special combat cycle that is produced in advanced hidden facilities on middle east and only delivered to the battlefield through parachute-dropping planes. This Armored bike comes ridden by a RPG soldier and is known to be way more resistant to machinegun and missiles. According to some unconfirmed rumours, replacing its rider by toxin-powered terrorists or Jarmen Kell has turn the tidal of many battles in favor of the ruthless Jackal forces.
