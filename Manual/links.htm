FluidPlay Studios is the game studio responsible for the Command and Conquer Boss Generals creation and development. Headed by Breno 'MaDDoX' Azevedo, with almost 20 years on the road, it's most probably the most experienced studio for RTS game projects still in activity. You'll find top quality RTS modification projects there, like the very successful HTAK (Hardcore TA:Kingdoms) and other new games in development like Total Atomic Power
